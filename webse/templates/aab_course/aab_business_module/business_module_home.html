Business and Accounting Module

In the business module, we study relevant business models in the green economy sector that combine digitalization and the most advanced economic models to achieve sustainability. First, we study the main characteristics of the market in which the company operates. Second, we study the company business model and the role of digitalization in that model. Third, an expert in the sector gives a talk about the business model that we study in each chapter.

The business and accounting module is organized in two blocks. First, we study the role that digitalization plays on sustainability. In particular, we study how digitalization could foster sustinability in cities and in the energy sector. In the second block, we cover some salient topics in accountability.


  • The first two chapters study the role of digitalization to achieve sustainability in cities and in the energy sector.
  • The last chapter of the course focuses on some salient topics in accountability.
