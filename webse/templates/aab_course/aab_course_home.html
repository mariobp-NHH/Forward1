Auditing, Accounting and Business Course

This course studies auditing, accounting, and the role of digitalization in the most advanced business models in the green economy sector.

We study the main features of different companies that aim to increase sustainability, and we study their business models that connect digitalization and green economy.

App Development Module

The app development module is organized in three blocks that complement each other. First, the students design the layout of the app by using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Flask. Second, they program the economic simulations by using Python. Finally, they deploy their apps in the server, by using GitGub, Heroku, and Amazon Web Services.

Business Module

In the business module, we study relevant business models in the green economy sector that combine digitalization and the most advanced economic models to achieve sustainability. First, we study the main characteristics of the market in which the company operates. Second, we study the company business model and the role of digitalization in that model. Third, an expert in the sector gives a talk about the business model that we study in each chapter.
