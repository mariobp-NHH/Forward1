Auditing, Accounting and Business Course

This course studies auditing, accounting, and the role of digitalization in the most advanced business models in the green economy sector.

We study the main features of different companies that aim to increase sustainability, and we study their business models that connect digitalization and green economy.

App Development Module

The app development module is organized in three blocks that complement each other. First, the students design the layout of the app by using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Flask. Second, they program the economic simulations by using Python. Finally, they deploy their apps in the server, by using GitGub, Heroku, and Amazon Web Services.

Business Module

In the business module, we study relevant business models in the green economy sector that combine digitalization and the most advanced economic models to achieve sustainability. First, we study the main characteristics of the market in which the company operates. Second, we study the company business model and the role of digitalization in that model. Third, an expert in the sector gives a talk about the business model that we study in each chapter.

Frequently Asked Questions

Bergen municipality and Skyss are concerned about the air quality in the city produced by transport, and they want to introduce new functionalities in the Skyss App to abate transport carbon emissions and to increase air quality. Write an essay, no longer than six pages, in which you give advice to Bergen municipality and Skyss to help them to introduce those functionalities considering the next points. Those points are only illustrative, you can customize your essay based on the solutions that you propose. You have freedom to choose the point that you consider relevant.

  1. Which are the functionalities that could be implemented in the app to abate transport carbon emissions?
  2. Which is the target group for those functionalities?
  3. How will you incentivize the use of those functionalities?
  4. Which could be the main challenges from a social, economic, and technical perspective?
  5. Could you provide some cost estimation to introduce those functionalities?
  6. If you consider it relevant, design a Canvas Business Model to address these points.

The essay must be submitted by using WiseFlow within December 19.
