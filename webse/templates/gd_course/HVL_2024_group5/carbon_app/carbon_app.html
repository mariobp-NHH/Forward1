Carbon App Calculator

Welcome to the Carbon App, your essential tool for analyzing and mitigating your carbon emissions. In today's world, where environmental sustainability is of paramount importance, it's crucial to be mindful of our carbon footprint and its impact on the planet. By utilizing the Carbon App, you not only gain insights into your personal carbon emissions but also contribute to the collective effort towards a greener future. Begin by selecting your mode of transportation. Input the distance you've traveled in kilometers. Select the type of fuel utilized by your transportation and analyze the results.
