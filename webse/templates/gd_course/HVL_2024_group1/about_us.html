About us

CarbonCare aims to address Europe’s significant carbon emissions from transportation by advocating for the adoption of lower-emission modes. Our initiative introduces a carbon emissions application, designed to raise individual awareness and facilitate the calculation of carbon footprints. Through analysis of various transportation modes, including cars, airplanes, ferries, trains, motorcycles, and buses, we emphasize the importance of informed choices in reducing environmental impact. CarbonCare is designed to encourage collective action to reduce carbon emissions.

Our business model is founded on the increasing significance of opting for sustainable choices.
