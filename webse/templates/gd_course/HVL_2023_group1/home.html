Welcome to the Carbon App!

We developed this app to help you reduce your carbon footprint by providing you with an easy way to calculate your carbon emissions. Our app is user-friendly, and it gives you an overview of your carbon footprint based on the data you input. By using our app, you can gain a better understanding of what affects your carbon footprint and what you can do to reduce it! We hope that our app can help you make the right choices to reduce your carbon footprint and contribute to combating climate change. Thank you for using our app!

Developers

Our team of students of economics has this semester taken the class of Green Digitalization and App Development. The teacher Mario Blázquez de Paz has been teaching the students to develop and deploy their own app in groups. The programming languages the students have used is HTML, CSS, Flask, Latex, Python and JavaScript. The students have learned how to deploy their app on Amazon web Service and Heroku. The students have worked together with this app in Github. If you have any questions about the app or the developers, you are welcome to ask questions to the group or contact the members on their LinkedIn profiles.
