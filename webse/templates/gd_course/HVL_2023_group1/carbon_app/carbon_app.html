Carbon App Calculator

Our app aims to help reduce carbon emissions in Bergen, and provide app users and Bergen Municipality with valuable insights on how to reduce carbon emissions. The app will let the municipality and its citizens cooperate better when aiming to reduce transport emissions. The app will also provide the municipalities with potentially valuable data for further research on the field. We can differentiate our app from competitors by implementing features such as notifying users in periods of high emissions to encourage them to choose trips with lower emission, and implement features that lets them compare their carbon emissions to the average person's. We believe these features will benefit municipalities in reducing emissions and encourage them to adopt our app.
