View chat (main chat):

We use this chat to solve questions related to programming, to deploy the app, to install packages, to design the app, methodology, i.e., any question about the topics covered in the chapters of the course. The chat is open to all the participants in the course. We use the chat in a regular basis to exchange information about the “weekly challenge,” and any doubts about the material covered in the lectures. I encourage all the groups (students) to participate actively in the chat and to help each other. I will participate actively in the chat trying to make the course more useful for the students.
