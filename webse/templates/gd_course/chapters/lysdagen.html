Lysdagen Conference

In the Lysdagen conference, the students present their economic models, their business models, and their apps. The students have built very advanced economic models to work out transport carbon emissions. They have designed innovative business models to abate those emissions. Those business models vary from the promotion of sustainable cities, the reduction of carbon emissions among youth population, specific target groups and companies, and business models that aim to foster the adoption of a carbon offset market. Finally, they have combined different programming languages to develop innovative apps with a solid software architecture foundation.

The Lysdagen foster the collaboration between the university and the society. To connect the work developed by the students with the society, the Startup Lab and Bergen municipality will share with the students their projects in digitalization and sustainability.
