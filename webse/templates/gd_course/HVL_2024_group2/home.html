Our Calculator tool is designed to help you understand and reduce your carbon footprint by providing insights into your transportation choices. Here's how it works:

You'll start by selecting your preferred mode of transportation and entering the distance you travel using that mode. Our app will then calculate your carbon emissions based on your input.

After you've entered your data, our app will generate a comprehensive graph showing your total emissions and the breakdown of emissions by transportation mode. This will give you a clear overview of your environmental impact and help you identify areas where you can make greener choices.

Thank you for using our app! We hope it empowers you to make more sustainable lifestyle choices.
