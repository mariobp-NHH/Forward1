Sustainable Energy Platform

The Sustainable Energy Platform aims to achieve a sustainable energy future by hosting the most advanced research in the fields of energy market design, sustainable energy, and climate change.

To achieve a sustainable energy future, the platform not only hosts research, but also dashboards and databases that provide salient information in that field in real time.

To facilitate the discussion between researchers, students and practitioners, the platform provides different forums where the users can share information and discuss relevant topics. To foster the dissemination and to facilitate the formation of the students, the papers contain a section with questions where the students can check their understanding of the papers.
