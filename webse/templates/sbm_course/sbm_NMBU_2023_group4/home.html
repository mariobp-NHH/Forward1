Welcome to The Carbon App

Carbon app for you who commute. This app is made for you who must travel every day to get where you need to be. Our mission is to help you measure your environmental impact. This app lets you find the route with the least environmental impact. You can also build a network and find others who commute the same way as you. Add them and schedule a carpool.
