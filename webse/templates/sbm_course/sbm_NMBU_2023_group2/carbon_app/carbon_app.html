Carbon App Calculator

Using our carbon calculator focused on travel-related emissions is a straightforward process aimed at providing you as a user with insights into your transportation-related carbon footprint. In our calculator you will find fields prompting you to input details about your modes of transportation and distances traveled. Begin by writhing in your estimated travel distance (km) with said transportation method, and simply let the website calculate your travel emission in too CO2.

Here is a step by step guide on how to use our calculator!

  1. Begin by writhing in your estimated travel distance in km with said transportation method.
  2. Click on the "Submit" button and simply let the website calculate your travel emission in too CO2.
