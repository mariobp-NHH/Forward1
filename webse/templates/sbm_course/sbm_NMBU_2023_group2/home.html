Our Team

Our team of student entrepreneurs has joined forces to develop a carbon-oriented app aimed at helping users calculate their carbon emissions. In the face of escalating climate change concerns, the team is driven by a shared mission to bridge the gap between awareness and action. Recognizing the pivotal role of individual contributions, the app intends to empower users by providing a user-friendly platform to measure and monitor their carbon footprint. By translating environmental consciousness into tangible impact, the team envisions their app as a catalyst for positive change, inspiring a community of environmentally conscious individuals to collectively address the urgent challenges of climate change.
