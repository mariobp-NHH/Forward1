Welcome to the Carbon App!

Thank you for using our app! We developed this app to assist you reducing your carbon footprint. By providing an easy way to calculate your carbon emmisions, you can contribute to the climate change in a positive way. You will gain a better understanding of the factors influencing your carbon footprint and learn actionable steps to decrease it. It is important that governments, businesses and individuals all around the world have a higher focus in decreasing emissions for the greater good. This means gaining a better understanding of which emissions are a significant contributor to global climate change. In addition, increase the use of renewable energy and adopt low-carbon lifestyles. We hope that our app empowers you to make informed choices, ultimately contributing to the battle against climate change.
